# Brand Guidelines — AtyrePrint

## Brand Identity

**Name:** AtyrePrint
**Tagline:** Custom Clothing & Gifts that Speak for You
**Industry:** Print-on-demand & embroidery services
**Location:** Notting Hill, London, United Kingdom

## Logo Usage

- Primary logo: Forest green on white background
- Reversed logo: White on forest green background
- Minimum clear space: Equal to the height of the "A" in AtyrePrint
- Never stretch, rotate, or recolour the logo outside approved palettes
- Logo file: `web/public/images/icons/logo.svg`

## Typography

### Fonts

| Font | Usage | Weight Range |
|---|---|---|
| **Inter** | Body text, UI elements, navigation | 400 (regular), 500 (medium), 600 (semibold), 700 (bold) |
| **Playfair Display** | Headings, hero text, section titles | 600 (semibold), 700 (bold) |

### Type Scale

| Element | Font | Size (mobile → desktop) | Weight |
|---|---|---|---|
| H1 (Hero) | Playfair Display | 2.25rem → 3.75rem | Bold |
| H2 (Section Title) | Playfair Display | 1.875rem → 2.25rem | Bold |
| H3 (Card Title) | Inter | 1.25rem | Semibold |
| Body | Inter | 1rem | Regular |
| Small / Caption | Inter | 0.875rem | Regular |
| Label / Overline | Inter | 0.75rem | Medium, uppercase, tracking-wider |

## Tone of Voice

- **Warm & approachable** — We're a small team that cares
- **Professional** — We know our craft
- **Encouraging** — "Let's create something special"
- **Confident** — "Quality speaks for itself"

Avoid: overly corporate language, jargon, pushy sales copy.

## Photography Style

- Clean, well-lit product photography on white or neutral backgrounds
- Lifestyle shots showing products in use (worn, held, in settings)
- Close-up detail shots for embroidery and print quality
- Consistent colour treatment that complements the green/gold palette
- No cluttered or busy backgrounds

## UI Patterns (from Codezeel/Kidupia template)

These layout patterns are adapted from the reference template:

- **Hero Section:** Full-width with overlaid text, CTA buttons, optional carousel
- **Category Grid:** Rounded cards with images and product count
- **Product Cards:** Image, title, price, rating, quick-add button
- **Trust Banner:** Stats bar (years experience, continents, products, guarantee)
- **Testimonials:** Carousel with avatar, quote, name, role
- **Newsletter:** Full-width section with email input and CTA
- **Footer:** Multi-column with logo, links, contact info, social icons
